    Mind Meditation App

    IOS App Design - UX Design

    The Why

    I have created the Mind Meditation App as a way for people to connect to their inner self on a deeper level and experience meditation through a functionally easy to use mobile application

    What is Mind?

    Mind is an app designed to foster beneficial meditation practices through various activities. Users earn points by meditating daily, which they can spend on objects to enhance their 3D spaceâ€”a low poly environment. The app incorporates UX principles to maximize user engagement.


    • Streamline daily activities
    • Short and meaningful experiences
    • Easy to start and use the app
    • Gamification and building your space is rewarding
    • Ability to work towards multiple goals at once

    Key Areas of Practice

    So I designed tranquil low poly 3D spaces in Blender, featuring calming abstract geometry. These spaces animate during activities like spinning, offering users visual progress feedback.

    Along with similar low poly 3D objects the user can use to customise their space to their liking in the 3D space editor within the app.

    Target Audience

    My research targets age groups less inclined to meditate, probing their needs and desires for self-care. Older millennials emerge as the primary focus, with only 42% meditating weekly compared to an average of 52% among younger adults in my generational cohort study.

    3D Spaces

    Feedback from early user testing prompted the inclusion of a customizable 3D space. Users expressed a desire for additional engagement beyond meditation alone, citing a need for more immediate gratification.

    Research into user engagement strategies led me to explore gamification techniques. I aimed to create a progression loop where users are rewarded for activities, triggering dopamine release and fostering continued engagement. This led to the implementation of a point system within the app.

    Achievements

    The Mind application also contains achievements which are obtaineed via in app challenges like Meditating a certain amount of times, participating in Breathwork activities a number of timres and more. These achievements provide the user points upon completion for them to spend in the apps 3D space store.

    Home

    A simple but very intuitive design using white space, contrast and UX laws such as The Ziegarnik & Goal Gradient Effect to keep users engaged and coming back.

    Statistics

    Users can easily keep track of their stats for different activities and change or add goals on the fly using the Goal Gradient and Ziegarnik Effect again to boost motivation.

    Rewards

    Easy to use and engaging rewards screen where users can claim rewards and earn points for objects to ue in their 3D space.

    Profile

    Users can view and edit their space easily and buy objects that they are able to then place into their 3D space.
