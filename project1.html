    Mind Meditation App

    IOS App Design - UX Design

    What is Mind

    Mind is a application aimed at giving people a space to work on Mindfulness through gamification. Mind does this by tasking users to complete daily activities and recieving points that they can then spend on customising their 3D space.

    Activites

    Mind uses daily mindful activities as a way for users to practice Mindfulness and receive the benefits from the practice.

    Activities are created from each type of mindful practice like, breathwork, pausing, positive video, movement and meditation. After completing an activity Mind rewards the user with some points in which they can spend via the in-app store to customise their 3D space.

    Statistics

    Mind provides the user with full detailed Statistics on each of their activities and goals through a weekly recap graphic

    The Statistics page is key for users to view their progress and success in completing mindfull activities. Here the user is able to set goals for each kind of mindful activity, view their progress and keep track of their weekly recap.

    Acheivements

    Acheivements are more rewards earned by completing a number of various activities a certain amount of time and are another way for users to earn points.

    Acheivements are rewarded to users upon completion and include more spendable points. Acheivements are another way of getting users to become more engaged with the Mind app and having another goal to work towards.

    3D Customisable Spaces

    The 3D Customisable spaces are what really makes Mind different from other applications. As these spaces are created for users to make their own and work towards their desired 3D space layout & look.
    These spaces were created using a low poly technique which is not only visually appealing but also calming for the user, these spaces are essentially a large island which the user can place various 3D assets bought from the in-app store.

    3D Assets

    The 3D assets is what the user will be working towards most of the time on their journey, these 3D assets below have been created in a low poly style and are purchasable from the in-app store via earned points.
    These assets can be placed however the user pleases in their space. These assets include objects like trees, rocks and misc objects.

    App Overview

    Home

    The landing page for the Mind app and where users can access daily activities, earned points and their profile.

    Statistics

    A in-depth Statistics screen for users to keep track of their progress, make goals and check their weekly recaps.

    Acheivements

    An simple screen which displays the users acheivements they have obtained and are close to completing.

    3D Space Editor

    The screen where users use their purchased assets to place in their space and customise it however they please.
