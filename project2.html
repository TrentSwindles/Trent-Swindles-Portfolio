    Abode Website

    Web Design - UX Design

    The Why

    First home buyers in Auckland are least likely than ever to buy a house in the current market, how can a informative website help this struggling group?

    What is Abode?

    Abode is a website tailored for first home buyers in Auckland, NZ, amidst an unprecedented housing market. With affordability challenges rampant, Abode empowers buyers with a comprehensive 5-step program. This includes actionable advice from qualified professionals, alongside a wealth of resources and contacts to facilitate successful home purchases in the current market.


    • Easy to understand information
    • 5-Step Program
    • Ability to talk to professionals
    • Vast resources
    • One easy to use website

    Target Audience

    I am focusing on 20-30 year olds and first home buyers because there is a clear need for these groups to get them selves into the housing market before it becomes to late in their lives.

    In 2021, a study found that 8 out of 10 millennials feel homeownership is out of reach, with 7 in 10 losing hope of ever owning a home. Similarly, 7 in 10 view stopping rent payments as the main motivation for buying property, reflecting concerns over rising rent and house prices. According to the NZ Herald, many young people see themselves as a generation of renters rather than owners.

    5-Step Plan

    Abode's flagship feature is a 5-step plan designed to educate first home buyers on preparing for, navigating, and completing their first home purchase. This comprehensive plan incorporates real-world insights from qualified real estate agents, mortgage advisors, and brokers, providing invaluable guidance to help buyers enter the housing market confidently.

    • Determine your non-negotiables
    • Guide to open homes
    • Finance
    • Buying
    • Moving in
