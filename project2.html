    Abode Website

    Web Design - UX Design

    What is Abode?

    Abode is a website tailored for first home buyers in Auckland, NZ, amidst an unprecedented housing market. With affordability challenges rampant, Abode empowers buyers with a comprehensive 5-step program. This includes actionable advice from qualified professionals, alongside a wealth of resources and contacts to facilitate successful home purchases in the current market.


    • Easy to understand information
    • 5-Step Program
    • Ability to talk to professionals
    • Vast resources
    • One easy to use website

    5-Step Plan

    Abode's main feature is a 5-step plan designed to educate first home buyers on preparing for, navigating, and completing their first home purchase. This comprehensive plan incorporates real-world insights from qualified real estate agents, mortgage advisors, and brokers, providing invaluable guidance to help buyers enter the housing market confidently.

    • Determine your non-negotiables
    • Guide to open homes
    • Finance
    • Buying
    • Moving in

    Blog

    The Abode website also features a content rich blog with tips and tricks on house keeping, like de-cluttering and house maintenance. The blog also features great articles from real industry insights around the housing market and first home purchasing. The blog is a great place for new users of Abode to visit for more information.

    • Tips & Tricks articles
    • Updates around the housing market
    • House Keeping articles
    • Real Industry Insights

    Contact

    Another feature that separates Abode from other real estate informational websites is the ability to connect with real people who are working in the industry and are able to provide industry insights to users via contacting.

    • Real Estate Agents
    • Mortgage Brokers
    • Mortgage Advisors
