    Parksafe Website

    Web Design - UX Design

    The Why

    Belmont, Pukekohe has a serious parking issue so how can an online parking service help residents of the effected area?

    What is Parksafe?

    Parksafe addresses a pressing parking problem in the Pukekohe Belmont area, where limited parking spots pose safety concerns. The website/service provides residents with a secure parking solution. Users can create accounts on the website to book parking spots for themselves and visitors, ensuring a safer parking experience in the neighborhood.


    • One easy to use website
    • Safe parking solution
    • Secure booking
    • Simple booking process
    • Simple sign up process

    Target Audience

    I will be focusing on a 25-35 year old demographic as this is the demographic that is in the first home buyer market and younger families. As when I was living in the area there was a very significant amount of youn families and first home buyers most likely aged from 25-35.

    Target Audience Interviews

    What are some issues you currently experience around Pukekohe?
    Parking, public transport and traffic

    How does this issue impact you? (Please answer in regards to your main issue)
    Parking impacts me the most especially around the area where I live, there is very limited parking due to small roads and yellow lined streets. This is a issue when I want to have friends, family and guests over due to finding parking. It also results in people parking on their front lawns which isn't a good look.

    How do you think this issue impacts Pukekohe as a whole?
    This issue impacts Pukekohe as a whole because it makes Pukekohe look less organized and messier because cars are just about everywhere on these streets.

    Booking Process

    In order for the Parksafe service to be successful I had to create a intuitive and visually appealing booking process for users to regularly engage in. After the user had a account and registered their vehicle they will be able to choose from 3 different parking areas around Belmont, Pukekohe where they are able to then easily book a park, take a look.

    Home

    Very simple and to the point home screen where the user can book their park very easily.

    Booking

    Straight forward booking system where the user can choose their park in their desired area.

    Booked Park

    After booking a park the user will be greeted with this screen as booking confirmation.

    Menu

    Simple and simplistic navigation where the user is able to simply navigate to different areas of the website.
